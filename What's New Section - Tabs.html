4 FACs to 5 FACs

We hope you're excited as we are about introducing a new 5 FAC model*. The Facilitators will continue to hold the same responsibilities as they have in previous years, and the 5th Facilitator will also serve as the primary TL Supervisor.

*5 FAC Model applies to stateside sites only.

2 TLs to 3 TLs

Just as we are moving to a new model for Facilitators, we are now going to provide a 3 TL model. All TLs will have an entirely new daily schedule.

New Pay Structure

We are introducing a brand new pay structure! All Team Leaders, Wellness Persons, Office Coordinators, and Logistics Coordinators are now paid an hourly wage. Facilitators, and Site Counselors are professional exempt, while Site Administrators are administrative exempt.
