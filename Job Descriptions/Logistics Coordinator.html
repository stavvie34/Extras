SuperCamp Logistics Coordinators are responsible for ensuring that all logistical systems are in place, as well as keeping track of all the logistical duties. Logistics Coordinators possess the following qualities and talents; they the reason people count on them to create and maintain professional, impeccable, well-organized environments at camp:

Receptive

Timely

Personal

Energetic

Job Description

Before Camp

Training

Participate in training conference calls (2-3 throughout May-June).

Manual

Thoroughly read and review the Logistics Coordinator manual.

Love Notes

Create staff “Love Notes” (materials sent from Oceanside).

During Camp

Supplies

Direct the unpacking of camp supplies (from storage unit to site).

Prepare

Ensure supplies for each day are prepared well in advance.

Set-Up

Manage the physical set-up of SuperCamp spaces (Mainroom and Courseroom)

Organize

Organize and maintain supplies of camp in drawer systems.

Inventory

Complete pre-camp inventory and appropriately distribute camp supplies.

Impeccablility

Maintain impeccability of SuperCamp environment.

Arrival/Departure

Support office staff with preparations for camper arrival day and departure day.

Outdoor Adventure Day

Assist with logistics of Outdoor Adventure Day.

Organize

Manage and organize TL assignments and afternoon activity sign ups.

Facilitate

Facilitate logistics portions of staff meetings.

Support

Quality

Work with the entire site team to assure the quality delivery of all curriculum.

Impeccability

Set up and maintain the impeccability of all course rooms and meeting spaces.

Social Media

Participate in the taking of pictures, video, and posting of social media for Grad Support.

Preparation

Prepare all materials needed to create a safe and success driven learning environment.

Assistance

Provide context and assistance to any other role in delivering excellent customer service.

Job Requirements

Qualities

Education

Bachelor’s Degree or higher.

Experience

Experience in teaching youth.

Communication

Professional in your written and verbal communication.

Presence

Vibrant and engaging front-of-the-room presence.

Integrity

Integrity, honesty, kindness, humility, patience, and a genuine concern for others.

Management

Effective classroom management skills.

Fitness

Able to lift at least 20 lbs.

Guidance

Willing to accept guidance, direction, and supervision from manager & peers.

Judgment

Sound judgment, able to effectively problem solve, and the ability to build rapport through your leadership.

Curriculum

Able to memorize and deliver large amounts of curriculum with accuracy.

Availability

Training

Available to attend in-person training (Paid for by QLN). Know It By Heart – Wednesday, March 29th – Sunday, April 2nd 2017 and Paving The Way – (Dates TBD)

Summer Availability

Available during summer for placement (June through August)

Try-Out

Willing to try out in-person or through video tryout.

Relationship

Desire a long-term relationship with Quantum Learning Network.

This position is professional exempt.
